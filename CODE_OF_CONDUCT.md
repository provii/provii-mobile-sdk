# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Participants are expected to uphold professional conduct in all interactions. Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, and trolling are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to conduct@provii.app. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.

Reports will be handled confidentially. The project maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct, and to ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for behaviours deemed inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
